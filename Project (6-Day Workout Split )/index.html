Weekly Gym Workout Plan

  • Monday - Chest
  • Tuesday - Back
  • Wednesday - Shoulders
  • Thrusday - Arms (Biceps + Triceps)
  • Friday - Legs
  • Saturday - Abs & Core + Weak Point Training

Monday - Chest

  1. Bench Press – 4×6–8
  2. Incline Dumbbell Press – 4×8–10
  3. Chest Dips (weighted if possible) – 3×10–12
  4. Cable Fly / Pec Deck – 3×12–15
  5. Push-ups (burnout set) – 2×to failure

Tuesday - Back

  1. Deadlift – 4×5
  2. Pull-Ups / Lat Pulldown – 4×8–10
  3. Seated Cable Row – 3×10–12
  4. Face Pulls – 3×12–15
  5. Barbell Row – 4×8–10

Wednesday - Shoulders

  1. Overhead Press (barbell or dumbbell) – 4×6–8
  2. Arnold Press – 3×8–10
  3. Lateral Raises – 4×12–15
  4. Front Raises – 3×12–15
  5. Rear Delt Fly (machine or dumbbell) – 3×12–15
  6. Shrugs – 3×12–15

Thrusday - Arms (Biceps + Triceps)

Biceps :

  1. Barbell Curl – 4×8–10
  2. Dumbbell Hammer Curl – 3×10–12
  3. Preacher Curl / Cable Curl – 3×12–15

Triceps :

  1. Close Grip Bench Press – 4×6–8
  2. Skull Crushers – 3×8–10
  3. Rope Pushdowns – 3×10–12
  4. Overhead Dumbbell Extension – 3×12–15

Friday - Legs

  1. Squats – 4×6–8
  2. Romanian Deadlift – 4×8–10
  3. Walking Lunges – 3×12–15 each leg
  4. Leg Press – 4×10–12
  5. Leg Curls – 3×12–15
  6. Calf Raises – 4×15–20

Saturday - Abs & Core + Weak Point Training

  1. Hanging Leg Raises – 4×12–15
  2. Cable Crunch – 3×15
  3. Russian Twists – 3×20
  4. Plank Variations – 3×1 min hold
  5. Optional: Train any lagging muscle group (extra arms, chest, or shoulders)
